题解：SP5971 LCMSUM - LCM Sum

Created2025-10-09|Updated2025-10-09|题解
|Post Views:

题目让你求：

∑i=1nlcm⁡(i,j)\sum_{i = 1}^n \operatorname{lcm}(i, j) i=1∑n​lcm(i,j)

推式子：

=∑i=1ni,ngcd⁡(i,n)= \sum_{i = 1}^n \frac{i, n}{\gcd(i, n)} =i=1∑n​gcd(i,n)i,n​

=n∑i=1nigcd⁡(i,n)= n \sum_{i = 1}^n \frac{i}{\gcd(i, n)} =ni=1∑n​gcd(i,n)i​

=n∑d∣n∑i=1nid[gcd⁡(i,n)=d]= n \sum_{d | n} \sum_{i = 1}^{n} \frac{i}{d}[\gcd(i, n)=d] =nd∣n∑​i=1∑n​di​[gcd(i,n)=d]

=n∑d∣n∑i=1n/di[gcd⁡(id,n)=d]= n \sum_{d | n} \sum_{i = 1}^{n/d} i[\gcd(id, n)=d] =nd∣n∑​i=1∑n/d​i[gcd(id,n)=d]

=n∑d∣n∑i=1n/di[gcd⁡(i,nd)=1]= n \sum_{d | n} \sum_{i = 1}^{n/d} i[\gcd(i, \frac{n}{d})=1] =nd∣n∑​i=1∑n/d​i[gcd(i,dn​)=1]

=n∑d∣n∑i=1di[gcd⁡(i,d)=1]= n \sum_{d | n} \sum_{i = 1}^{d} i[\gcd(i, d)=1] =nd∣n∑​i=1∑d​i[gcd(i,d)=1]

=n∑d∣ndϕ(d)2= n \sum_{d | n} \frac{d\phi(d)}{2} =nd∣n∑​2dϕ(d)​

到这里就直接可以预处理每个nnn的约数dϕ(d)2\displaystyle \frac{d\phi(d)}{2}2dϕ(d)​值和了。
